Senna: MP4/4 Livery

May 05, 2024.


While getting into Formula 1, I learned more about McLaren's history. Ayrton Senna is one of the best Formula 1 drivers of all time. However, his life was cut short by his profession. To honor the 30 anniversary of his passing, I made a livery in Forza Horizon 5 of the McLaren MP4/4, the car that Senna used to drive.
